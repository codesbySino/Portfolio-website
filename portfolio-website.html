About Me

I'm a product designer passionate about creating meaningful objects that enhance daily life. With a background in industrial design and a love for craftsmanship, I approach each project as an opportunity to solve problems through thoughtful design.

My work spans furniture, lighting, and decorative objects, always with an emphasis on sustainability and user experience. Based in Toronto, I draw inspiration from the city's diverse culture and natural surroundings.
